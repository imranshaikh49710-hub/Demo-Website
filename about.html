Our Story

20 Years of Painting Excellence

KsisPainter was founded in 2005 by Rajesh Ksis with a simple mission — to bring world-class painting quality to Indian homes at honest prices. Starting with just 3 painters in a small workshop in Thane, we've grown into a 50+ member team serving Mumbai, Thane, and Pune.

Over the years, we've completed 2,000+ projects ranging from 1BHK apartments to large commercial complexes. Our commitment to quality, transparency, and on-time delivery has made us one of Maharashtra's most trusted painting contractors.

We are certified applicators for Asian Paints, Berger, Dulux, and Nerolac — which means you get genuine premium products every time, with the manufacturer's backing.
